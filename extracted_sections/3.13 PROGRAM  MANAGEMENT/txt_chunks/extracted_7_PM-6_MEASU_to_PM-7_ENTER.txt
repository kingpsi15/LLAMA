mControl:  Develop, monitor, and report on the results of information security and privacy
hmeasures of performance.
Discussion:  Measures of performance are outcome-based metrics used by an organization to :
measure the effectiveness or efficiency of the information security and privacy programs and the o

.as defined in the risk management strategy. 6
